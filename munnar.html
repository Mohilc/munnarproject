EXPLORE THE MUNNAR

Munnar is a picturesque hill station located in the Idukki district of Kerala, India.Situated at an elevation of around 1,600 meters (5,200 feet) above sea level in the Western Ghats mountain range, Munnar is often referred to as the “Kashmir of South India.” Its breathtaking beauty, vast tea plantations, winding roads, and mist-covered viewpoints make it a popular travel destinatione,specially for honeymooners.
